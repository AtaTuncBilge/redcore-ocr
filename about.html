how this started

At work, we had a bunch of invoice PDFs that needed data extracted — product descriptions, prices, quantities, that kind of thing. Doing it manually was slow and painful.

So I started experimenting with Tesseract.js to see if I could automate it. First it was just a messy script, then it became a basic tool, and eventually it turned into what you see here.

The whole thing runs client-side because that was the simplest approach — no server to maintain, no data to worry about, just open the page and go.

the tool

Redcore OCR is a browser-based OCR tool. You upload a PDF or image, it runs text recognition locally using Tesseract.js, and gives you the results. There's also an invoice-specific mode that tries to parse line items from Turkish invoices.

It's not perfect, but it works pretty well for what it does. And it's free.
